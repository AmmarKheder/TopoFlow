🎯 TEXTE POUR EXPLIQUER WIND SCANNING (ANGLAIS):
================================================================================

Simple Version (pour slide):

"Standard AI processes data in arbitrary order (row-by-row).
TopoFlow follows the wind direction (upwind → downwind).
This captures how pollution actually moves through the atmosphere."

Detailed Version (pour présentation orale):

"Pollution doesn't move randomly - it follows the wind. Traditional 
models ignore this physics and process spatial data in fixed order.

TopoFlow reorders patches based on wind direction: upwind regions 
are processed first, downwind regions last. This mimics real 
atmospheric transport.

Result: The model learns pollution dispersal patterns naturally, 
improving forecast accuracy."
